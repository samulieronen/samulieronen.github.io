Want to play a game?


Filler.

An game project where you need to create the best alogrihm to win the opponent.
You get a map and a piece from the filler_vm and your mission is to place the piece to the map.

The goal is to fill the board with as many pieces as you can.
Winner is the player with most valid placements.

Would you play safe or commence attack?

My approach was to surround the enemy completely and stop it from placing any pieces.
I did this by calculating the minimum distance
from my position to an enemy position using manhattan distance.
|distance| = |x₁ - x₂| + |y₁ - y₂|
